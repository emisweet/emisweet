Is Momo As Real As We're Making It?

You’ve heard of the classic viral social media challenges like the Cinnamon Challenge or the Ice Bucket Challenge. While they were all fun and games, and in some cases even helped raise awareness, internet challenges with today’s kids are possibly taking a dark twist. Challenges now are supposedly encouraging self-harm, teaching young children how to commit suicide, and threatening children if they don’t. Sounds like a parent’s worst nightmare? Yep, pretty much. But is this even actually happening or are we just giving evil people more ideas?
